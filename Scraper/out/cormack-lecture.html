Allan M. Cormack - Nobel Lecture

Early Two-Dimensional Reconstruction and Recent Topics Stemming from It

Allan M. Cormack held his Nobel Lecture on 8 December 1979, at Karolinska Institutet, Stockholm. He was presented by Professor Ulf Rudhe, member of the Nobel Committee for Physiology or Medicine.
