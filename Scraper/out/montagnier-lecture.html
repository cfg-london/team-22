Luc Montagnier - Nobel Lecture

25 Years after HIV Discovery: Prospects for Cure and Vaccine

Luc Montagnier delivered his Nobel Lecture on 7 December 2008 at Karolinska Institutet in Stockholm. He was introduced by Professor Björn Vennström, Member of the Nobel Committee for Physiology or Medicine.
