Carol W. Greider - Nobel Lecture

Telomerase Discovery: The Excitement of Putting Together Pieces of the Puzzle

Carol W. Greider delivered her Nobel Lecture on 7 December 2009 at Karolinska Institutet in Stockholm. She was introduced by Professor Thomas Perlmann, member of the Nobel Assembly and associate member of the Nobel Committee for Physiology or Medicine.
