Baruj Benacerraf - Nobel Lecture

The Role of MHC Gene Products in Immune Regulation and Its Relevance to Alloreactivity

Baruj Benacerraf held his Nobel Lecture on 8 December 1980, at Karolinska Institutet, Stockholm. He was presented by Professor Georg Klein of the Karolinska Medico-Chirurgical Institute.
