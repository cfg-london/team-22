Barbara McClintock - Nobel Lecture

The Significance of Responses of the Genome to Challenge

Barbara McClintock held her Nobel Lecture on 8 December 1983, at Karolinska Institutet, Stockholm. She was presented by Professor Nils Ringertz, Member of the Nobel Committee for Physiology or Medicine.
