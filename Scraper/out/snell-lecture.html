George D. Snell - Nobel Lecture

Studies in Histocompatibility

George D. Snell held his Nobel Lecture on 8 December 1980, at Karolinska Institutet, Stockholm. He was presented by Professor Georg Klein of the Karolinska Medico-Chirurgical Institute.
