Richard J. Roberts - Nobel Lecture

An Amazing Distortion in DNA Induced by a Methyltransferase

Richard J. Roberts held his Nobel Lecture on 8 December 1993, at Karolinska Institutet, Stockholm. He was presented by Ralf Pettersson, Member of the Nobel Committee for Physiology or Medicine.
