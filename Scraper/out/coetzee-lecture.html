J. M. Coetzee - Nobel Lecture

He and his man

J. M. Coetzee delivered his Nobel Lecture at the Swedish Academy, Stockholm, 7 December 2003. He was introduced by Horace Engdahl, Permanent Secretary of the Swedish Academy.
