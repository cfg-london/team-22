Patrick Modiano - Nobel Lecture

Patrick Modiano held his Nobel Lecture on 7 December 2014, at the Swedish Academy, Stockholm. He was introduced by Peter Englund, Permanent Secretary of the Swedish Academy. The lecture was delivered in French.
