2017 Nobel Lectures in Chemistry

The 2017 Nobel Lectures in Chemistry will be held on Friday 8 December 2017 11.20 a.m.-1.10 p.m., at the Aula Magna, Stockholm University.
Address: Aula Magna, Stockholm University, Frescativägen 6, Stockholm.
